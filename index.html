Noppayut Sriwatanasakdi (Mew); นภยุสม์ ศรีวัฒนศักดิ์ (มิว); ノッパユット　スリワタナサク (ミュー)

Taken in 2019

Hi, call me Mew for short. I am a Natural Language Processing Engineer at Square Enix AI & Arts Alchemy, a start-up under the Square Enix group focusing on R&D of products involving AI, computer graphics, and arts.

I learned about Concept drift in grad school, but it’s not that popular so I picked up Natural Language Processing (NLP) on my first job, and have been working on it ever since.

I earned my master’s degree in Information Science in 2018 from Osaka University, and bachelor’s in Computer Engineering in 2015 from Chulalongkorn University.
